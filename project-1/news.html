New Fakemon Game Incoming!

Victor Asecks, Aug 22, 2022

An open world Fakemon adventure coming to Console Switch on November 18, 2022

A new pair of Fakemon games is coming this fall. Fakemon Scarlet and Violet are set to launch for the Console Switch on Nov. 18, kicking off a brand-new generation for the beloved monster-catching series.

Like Sword and Shield before them, Fakemon Scarlet and Violet take players on a new journey in a vast, open-world region brimming with unfamiliar characters and never-before-seen Fakemon.
